# Notes & Reflections

Drop past journals, reflections, coaching notes, or personal writings here.

## File Naming

Use descriptive names:
- `journal_2023.md`
- `coaching_notes_2022.txt`
- `sabbatical_reflections_2021.md`
- `career_pivot_thoughts_2020.md`

## What to Upload

**Good candidates:**
- Personal journals or reflection logs
- Notes from coaching or therapy sessions
- Reflections from major life events (sabbaticals, exits, transitions, losses)
- Letters to yourself
- Post-mortems from failed projects or relationships
- Peak experience reflections (moments of clarity or flow)

**Not necessary:**
- Random meeting notes
- Task lists or to-dos
- Documents without meaningful reflection

## After Uploading

1. Skim uploaded notes to identify recurring themes
2. Extract key insights and add them to `memory.md`
3. Reference these notes when facing similar situations or decisions

## What to Look For

- **Recurring worries or fears** — What keeps showing up?
- **Peak moments** — When have you felt most alive or aligned?
- **Decision patterns** — How have you made (or avoided) hard choices?
- **Relationship insights** — What have you learned about how you show up in relationships?
- **Identity shifts** — How has your sense of self changed over time?

---

**Your past reflections are a mirror. Use them to see yourself more clearly.**
