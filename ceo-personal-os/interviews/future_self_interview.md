# Future Self Interview

A guided self-interview to clarify the life you're building toward and the person you're becoming.

**Time required:** 45-60 minutes

**When to use this:** When setting long-term goals, feeling unclear about direction, or needing to reconnect with your vision

**How to use this:** Imagine yourself in the future — 5, 10, or 20 years from now — having lived in full alignment with your values and vision. Answer these questions **as that future self**, speaking back to your current self.

## What to Do Next

After completing this interview:

1. **Compare your Future Self vision to your current goals.** Do they align? If not, adjust your goals.
2. **Write or update your Vivid Vision** (see `/frameworks/vivid_vision.md`) with the details that felt most true.
3. **Add key insights to `memory.md`** so you can reference this clarity in future reviews.
4. **Set one concrete action** to take in the next 7 days that moves you toward that future.
5. **Revisit this interview annually.** Your future self will evolve. That's normal and healthy.

---

## Final Note

Your future self is not a fantasy.

It's a possibility.

The gap between who you are now and who you could become is not a failure — it's a map.

Every decision you make either moves you toward that future or away from it.

This interview gives you clarity on the direction.

What you do with that clarity is up to you.
